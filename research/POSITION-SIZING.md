## 3. Dry-powder deployment triggers ($78k)
Deploy the muni-money-market reserve into the **best crowded chokepoints at better prices** when **either** condition fires:
- **Drawdown trigger:** a ≥20–25% decline in the complex (the 2027–28 air-pocket) → deploy into **ASML, GEV, COPX, MP** in roughly equal tranches.
- **Confirmation trigger:** two consecutive quarters of hyperscaler capex guided **up** (the "Barclays says capex is too low" scenario) → deploy **immediately** into the highest-conviction **GEV / ASML**, rather than keep waiting for a dip that may not come.
- If neither fires within ~18 months, dollar-cost the reserve into PAVE/GRID so it isn't a permanent cash drag.

## 4. Rebalancing & trim discipline
- **Rebalance** annually, or whenever any position drifts **±25% from its target weight**. Rebalance tax-efficiently: direct new cash/dividends to underweight names first; do actual *selling* inside the IRA (tax-free).
- **Trim rule (mostly in the IRA):** trim ~1/3 of a single name if it (a) exceeds **2× cost basis AND re-rates above ~50× forward earnings**, or (b) breaches the $125k position cap.
- **Tax-loss harvesting (taxable):** if an ETF is down >10%, harvest and rotate to a *correlated-but-not-substantially-identical* fund for 31 days to avoid the wash-sale rule (e.g., copper miners ↔ a broad materials fund), only if it doesn't disrupt the thesis.
- **Dividends/distributions:** reinvest into the most underweight position (keeps you buying low) rather than auto-DRIP each holding.

## 5. Thesis-based exits (these are cyclicals — use signals, not hard % stops)
Cut or sharply reduce a position when its specific thesis breaks:
- **MP / LEU (policy-floor trades):** cut MP if China extends the export-control suspension **and** NdPr stays <$80/kg; cut LEU if Russia-enrichment sanctions thaw or SMR timelines slip materially.
- **GEV / turbines:** trim if turbine lead times roll over (slots opening pre-2029) — the scarcity is easing.
- **SMH / MU (compute):** trim the tactical compute sleeve if memory/CoWoS pricing rolls over in 2027 — that leg is cyclical-peak, not structural.
- **Whole sleeve:** on a rate spike or dollar surge, expect every Tier-1 cyclical to re-rate down together regardless of scarcity — that is the unhedged macro assumption baked into this plan. Size so you can hold through it.
