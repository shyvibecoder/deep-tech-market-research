# Robotics & Autonomous Physical Systems — Deep Dive (Wave 1)

**Core framing:** Most robot hardware commoditizing fast (motors, frames, basic actuators); a handful of inputs stay structurally scarce. Demos ≠ deployment (gap ~3–5 yrs per modality = the central insight). Treat vendor production/cost claims (Tesla, Figure) as LOW confidence.

## Roadmap
- 2025 high-water: Figure 02 ~1,250 hrs at BMW (supervised, single-station). Waymo ~2,500 vehicles, 500k–1M paid rides/wk ($126B valuation) — the one autonomy segment past the chasm (gated by capital, not tech).
- 2026: humanoid pilots not scale — realistic hundreds–low-thousands (NOT 50k–100k headlines). Commercial units $50k–$100k+ (sub-$30k is a scale claim).
- 2027–28: first genuine multi-unit multi-shift factory deployments.
- eVTOL: NO US type cert yet as of Q1 2026; Joby EIS realistically mid-late 2027.
- Defense FPV drones scaling now (Ukraine ~8M/yr; Anduril Arsenal-1).

## Structural scarcities (ranked by durability × how-soon)
1. **Rare-earth permanent magnets (Dy/Tb NdFeB) ex-China** — binds NOW, eases ~2030–31. 2–4kg/humanoid. China ~91% refined; April-2025 export controls on Dy/Tb disrupted Optimus. Heavy-REE separation + 17.8yr mine lead = hardest moat. Expressions: MP Materials (MP), USA Rare Earth (USAR), Lynas. **The consensus scarcity that's UNDERRATED.**
2. **Real-world dexterous-manipulation DATA** — binds 2026–2030 (the demo→deployment gate). NON-CONSENSUS #1. No internet of physical interactions; compounding fleet moat. Scale AI, Sensei (private); pre-IPO Physical Intelligence, Skild. Deployed-fleet owners (Figure, Tesla) capture data exhaust.
3. **Precision reducers / planetary roller screws** — binds 2026–2029, ERODING moat (Schaeffler integrated actuators substitute; Leaderdrive China entrants). Harmonic Drive (6324 JP), Nabtesco (6268 JP), HIWIN (TW). Don't conflate "binds 2026" with durable moat.
4. **Certification/regulatory throughput** — binds 2026–2030, a TIME scarcity (FAA bandwidth, fenceless-safety standards). Underpriced. First-cert holders (Joby).
5. **Service/integration/fleet-ops labor** — binds 2028–2033 (late but durable "last-mile"). System integrators, teleop platforms — mostly private.
6. **Battery density/multi-shift uptime** — soft persistent; relieved operationally (swap) by 2027–28, not a hard cliff.

## Non-consensus
Binding scarcity is DATA + DEXTERITY, not hardware. Reducers over-hyped as a moat (strong substitution). Certification mispriced (time scarcity). RE magnets the one consensus scarcity that's underrated (Dy/Tb + 17.8yr lead).

**Method caveat:** Some figures (2–4kg magnet/robot, 17.8yr lead, Humanoid-100 list) second-hand (WebFetch 403); vendor claims low-confidence.
