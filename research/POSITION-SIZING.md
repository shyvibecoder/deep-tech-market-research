## 2. Position caps & concentration (check before every buy)
- **Single stock cap:** ≤ $120k (8% of sleeve ≈ 2.3% of total $5.2mm). Largest single name = MU $90k ✓.
- **Single ETF cap:** ≤ $240k (16%). Largest = PAVE $225k ✓.

## 3. Dry-powder deployment triggers ($75k: $25k taxable + $50k IRA)
Deploy the reserve into the **best crowded chokepoints at better prices** when **either** fires:
- **Drawdown trigger:** ≥20–25% decline in the complex (the 2027–28 air-pocket) → deploy roughly equally into **ASML** (taxable $25k) and **GEV / COPX / MP** (IRA $50k).
- **Confirmation trigger:** two consecutive quarters of hyperscaler capex guided **up** → deploy **immediately** into highest-conviction **GEV / ASML**, rather than wait for a dip that may not come.
- If neither fires within ~18 months, dollar-cost the reserve into PAVE (taxable) / SMH (IRA) so it isn't a permanent cash drag.

## 4. Rebalancing & trim discipline (most of it tax-free in the IRA)
- **Rebalance** annually, or when any position drifts **±25% from target weight**. Direct new cash/dividends to underweight names first; do actual *selling* inside the IRA (tax-free).
- **Trim rule:** trim ~1/3 of a name if it (a) exceeds **2× cost basis AND re-rates above ~50× forward earnings**, or (b) breaches the $120k cap. All trimmable names live in the IRA by design.
- **Tax-loss harvesting (taxable anchors):** if PAVE/GRID/FIW/SHLD is down >10%, harvest and rotate to a correlated-but-not-substantially-identical fund for 31 days (avoid wash-sale), only if it doesn't disrupt the thesis.
- **Dividends:** reinvest into the most underweight position rather than auto-DRIP each holding.

## 5. Thesis-based exits (cyclicals — use signals, not hard % stops)
- **MP / LEU (policy-floor trades):** cut MP if China extends the export-control suspension **and** NdPr stays <$80/kg; cut LEU if Russia-enrichment sanctions thaw or SMR timelines slip materially.
- **GEV / turbines:** trim if turbine lead times roll over (slots opening pre-2029).
- **SMH / MU (compute):** trim the tactical compute sleeve if memory/CoWoS pricing rolls over in 2027.
- **Whole sleeve:** a rate spike or dollar surge re-rates every Tier-1 cyclical down together regardless of scarcity — the unhedged macro assumption. Size so you can hold through it.
