# Red-Team: Compute & Quantum

**Verdict:** Thesis 1 = late-cycle capex trade in a "structural scarcity" costume (~80% priced). Thesis 2 = a story not an investable basket (real scarcity un-tickerable).

## 1. ALREADY-PRICED
- LITE: +340% (2025), +166% (2026 YTD) — puck arrived, stadium on fire.
- COHR: +97% YTD. AVGO: +110%/+51%, fully discovered.
- NVTS: pure momentum, $10M/qtr revenue, 2026 guided DOWN to ~$36M.
- MU: FQ1'26 net income $5.24B vs $332M FQ3'24 (16x) — earnings caught up to story.
- Quantum pure-plays: P/S 109 (IONQ), 836 (RGTI), 791 (QBTS); insiders net-SOLD ~$931M since 2021.
- Semis/AI-optics = most-covered theme on earth; the "GPU→HBM→packaging→optics→power" migration IS the 2026 sell-side deck. You're buying after every leg front-run.

## 2. CAPEX-DIGESTION TAIL (worse than thesis admits)
- All 6 baskets are derivatives of ONE ~$600–690B hyperscaler capex number.
- Capex GROWTH: +51% (2026) → +13% (2027) → ~5% (2028) [Allianz]. Second derivative sharply negative in 2027; memory/packaging are second-derivative trades — de-rate when growth slows.
- Funding fragile: ~90% of OCF on capex, $108B debt 2025, ~$1.5T projected issuance; AI revenue ~$51B vs ~$660–690B capex; GPU-backed debt maturities 2026–27, first defaults expected 2027–28.
- Call: growth air-pocket by 2027–28 >50% likely (mechanical); 30–60% drawdown in leveraged names needs only the growth scare, not contraction.

## 3. SUBSTITUTION (binds loosening faster than claimed)
- **CoWoS un-binding by construction:** ~35k (late-24) → 120–130k wafers/mo (end-26) = 3.7x, + 240–270k/yr outsourced to Amkor/SPIL. Scarcity has 2026–27 expiry.
- **HBM: 3 suppliers + ~50% capacity adds** (Samsung 170k→250k). LEAST defensible scarcity; never stayed tight through a 50%-capacity cycle. Thesis ranking (HBM>CoWoS durability) is BACKWARDS.
- DDR5 squeeze (60–70% price hikes) is the SIGNAL that ends scarcity — high prices cure high prices.
- Optics: LPO cuts power ~50% keeping pluggable form factor (>8M 1.6T LPO ports by 2027) — substitutes CPO. CPO-2027–30 leg is WEAKEST part of extended optics call.
- ASIC-vs-GPU shifts demand shape (fewer HBM stacks per box in some designs).

## 4. QUANTUM REALITY CHECK (story not basket)
- Genuine binding layers (cryo-CMOS/wiring, QEC decoding/Riverlane) are PRIVATE/pre-IPO — can't own the bottleneck.
- ASPI: Q1'26 rev $4.2M (mostly LNG/radiopharma NOT Si-28), net loss $26.7M, Si-28 plant ships Q3 2026 — quantum optionality ~0% of P&L.
- OXIG: diversified industrial, quantum a small segment (~90% non-quantum).
- Pure-plays de-rating (RGTI −10%, QBTS −9% YTD) — validates "marginal through 2030." 2030 market estimates diverge 5x ($4.2B–$20.2B) = nobody knows.
- Value trap for the decade for tickerable proxies.

## 5. DOUBLE-COUNTING
Optical/InP (COHR/LITE/AVGO) in BOTH theses — same cos/fabs/AI-capex driver; quantum photonics is a rounding error on AI datacom. SiC/GaN (NVTS) in compute+frontier+energy — Navitas ~100% Nvidia-800V story = AI capex. Net: ONE macro bet (hyperscaler capex stays high), dressed as ~8 scarcities, ~1.0 correlation in drawdown.

## MISSED
- Reflexive funding loop (capital markets are the real bottleneck; circular Nvidia/OpenAI/Oracle vendor-financing; defaults 2027–28).
- Depreciation cliff (~$176B understated 2026–28 if GPU life 2–3yr not 5–6yr) → hits capex appetite.
- ASIC substitution as demand-SHAPE risk.
- Correlation/single-factor risk unacknowledged.
- **Power/grid is the ACTUALLY durable bottleneck** (multi-year permitting/interconnect CAN'T be quadrupled like CoWoS). Thesis points at relievable layers, underweights the unrelievable one.
- CXL memory pooling as DRAM-squeeze relief.

## RANKED most→least robust
1. MU (HBM) — real earnings but cyclical, own for earnings not moat. 2. CoWoS/TSMC — tight 2026, expiry after. 3. AVGO — diversified, but discovered. 4. Optics COHR/LITE — late-cycle, LPO risk. 5. NVTS — story stock. 6. Glass interposer — slippage risk. 7. Quantum (ASPI/OXIG) — value trap.

**One-liner:** Thesis 1 = real but CYCLICAL capex trade, ~80% priced, chokepoints actively widened — trade tactically don't hold as "structural." Thesis 2 = correct scarcity thesis with no honest way to own it before ~2030.
