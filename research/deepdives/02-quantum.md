# Quantum Technologies — Deep Dive (Wave 1)

**Core thesis:** Binding scarcities are NOT the qubits — they're the enabling layers that scale slower than qubit counts with physics/capital ceilings. Biggest risk: commercial quantum stays marginal through ~2030 (lab demos run ~5–8 yrs ahead of commercial value). Position picks-and-shovels, not pure-play qubit makers (down 28–33% YTD 2026, ~$31M rev on $10B valuations).

## Roadmap
- 2025: ~1,000–1,200 physical qubits; below-threshold error correction demonstrated (real). QuEra 96 logical qubits (Jan 2026 Nature).
- Vendor targets (treat as self-reported): IBM Starling 2029 (~200 logical qubits); Quantinuum Apollo 2030; IonQ 2M physical by 2030.
- Honest read: useful FT for real problems 2028–2032; broad commercial value early-mid 2030s.
- QKD range-capped (~100km), partly obsoleted by post-quantum crypto (software). Sensing = earliest real value but small market.

## Structural scarcities (ranked by how early/hard they bind)
1. **Helium-3 + sub-Kelvin cryogenics** — binds 2026–28, tightest 2028–32. He-3 physics-fixed supply (tritium decay), ~8–15k L/yr, $1,900–2,600/L. Dilution-fridge oligopoly (Bluefors ~34%, Oxford ~21–25%). Moat VERY HIGH. Expressions: **Oxford Instruments (LSE: OXIG)** cleanest public; Bluefors (private). Lunar He-3 (Interlune) speculative.
2. **Cryo-CMOS control electronics / I/O wiring bottleneck** — binds 2027–2030. The hardest ENGINEERING wall (can't wire millions of qubits). Largely private/pre-IPO (gap to front-run). Intel, SEEQC (private), Quantum Machines (private), Keysight (KEYS).
3. **Real-time QEC decoding (classical compute)** — binds 2028–2033 but MOST LIKELY TO DISSOLVE via algorithms (trap for moat investors). Riverlane (private pure-play), Q-CTRL, Nvidia (NVDA, CUDA-Q).
4. **QEC/quantum talent** — ~30k workers vs 250k roles by 2030; ~1 candidate/3 roles. Very durable. Favors incumbents (IBM, Google, Quantinuum).
5. **Specialty isotopes (Si-28, Yb, C-12)** — binds if silicon-spin scales (~2028+). ASP Isotopes (NASDAQ: ASPI) clean public pure-play.
6. **Photonic components (SNSPDs, InP lasers)** — binds now–2029; InP laser supply SHARED WITH AI OPTICS (Nvidia $4B lock-up stretched lead times). Lumentum (LITE), Coherent (COHR), GlobalFoundries (GFS).

## Non-consensus
Cryo-CMOS/wiring is most underappreciated hard wall (no obvious ticker = mispriced). Quantum photonics demand hostage to AI's optics shortage (shared InP supply chain). Decoding is a trap (likely solved). Over-hyped: lunar He-3, pure-play quantum equities, QKD.
