# Critical Minerals, Materials & Advanced Manufacturing — Deep Dive (Wave 1)

**Core thesis:** The bottleneck is almost never the ore — it's the PROCESSING/SEPARATION step, which China controls at 70–95%+ and has weaponized via export-licensing + tech-transfer bans. Distinguish PRICE CYCLES (lithium, nickel, cobalt — mean-reverting, substitutable) from STRUCTURAL CHOKEPOINTS (heavy-RE separation, magnet-making, gallium/germanium, enrichment SWU, copper). Mines take 10–20 yrs, refineries 5–7 — today's chokepoints locked in through early 2030s.

## KEY DATED CATALYST: China's Oct 2025 extraterritorial export-control escalation SUSPENDED to ~Nov 10–27, 2026 — a pause, not repeal. Discrete correlated re-escalation risk across REE/graphite/Ga/Ge/Sb simultaneously.

## Structural scarcities (ranked conviction × duration × non-substitutability)
1. **Heavy rare earths (Dy/Tb) + NdFeB magnets** — #1 chokepoint, binds now→2030+, DECADAL moat (81% of REE patents Chinese, 5–7yr learning curve, banned tech transfer). Lynas only ex-China HRE separator. Expressions: **MP Materials (MP)** (DoD $110/kg NdPr floor, Apple prepay), **Lynas (LYC)**, Neo (NEO); private Noveon, Cyclic, USA Rare Earth, Vulcan Elements.
2. **Uranium enrichment SWU/HALEU** — cleanest under-owned "AI power" derivative. Russian LEU ban Jan 1 2028 (hard cliff). Russia sole commercial HALEU. Expressions: **Centrus (LEU)** (only US-tech centrifuge+HALEU, $2.3B backlog), Cameco (CCJ), Urenco (private). DOE $2.7B deployed.
3. **Copper** — slowest, most certain. Supply peaks ~2030 (~33Mt), deficit ~30% by 2035. Binds 2028–2030, hard 2030–35. **Freeport (FCX)**, Ivanhoe (IVN), Teck (TECK), Southern Copper (SCCO).
4. **Gallium/Germanium** — China 98% Ga/91% Ge; byproduct INELASTICITY (can't scale independent of host metal). Acute on Nov-2026 lapse. Embedded plays (alumina/zinc w/ recovery), GaN demand pull.
5. **Graphite anode** — China >90% battery-grade. Syrah (SYR), Novonix (NVX), Westwater.
6. **Tungsten/Antimony** — China 83%/87%; US military 2027 zero-China deadline. Almonty, Perpetua, USAC.
7. **Semi specialty materials (photoresist)** — JAPAN ~90% EUV photoresist (TOK, JSR, Shin-Etsu); polysilicon Wacker+Hemlock ~75%.
8. **Lithium/Nickel/Cobalt** — CYCLES not structural scarcity (be skeptical). Li deficit 2026–35 is capex-cycle; Ni/Co oversupply.
9. **Additive manufacturing** — capability/secure-capacity bottleneck. Velo3D, 3D Systems.

## Non-consensus
HRE SEPARATION (not mining) is the prize. Magnet MAKING a second hidden chokepoint (China ~90% even where oxide ex-China). Enrichment SWU/HALEU the under-owned AI-power derivative. Copper deficit under-priced in severity/duration. Ga/Ge byproduct inelasticity. Nov-2026 suspension lapse = dateable correlated catalyst.

## Key risk
China's strongest weapon is the PRICE WAR not embargo — flooding market below Western breakeven. That's WHY DoD price floors ($110/kg NdPr) + offtakes are the load-bearing thesis, not geology. Own the processing chokepoint + demand government floors.
